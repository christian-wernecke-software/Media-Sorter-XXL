Data Processing

The core functionality of Media Sorter XXL processes all your media files locally on your computer. No photos, videos, or metadata are uploaded to our servers or any cloud service.

Reverse Geocoding (OpenStreetMap)

To provide the location-based sorting feature, the app sends GPS coordinates (Latitude/Longitude) to the Nominatim service (OpenStreetMap). This is necessary to retrieve city or town names. During this process, technical data (such as your IP address) is transmitted to OpenStreetMap. No other personal data is shared.
